About StudyBuddy

StudyBuddy is a platform designed to help students learn collaboratively by connecting those who need help with topics to peers who understand them well.

The idea is simple — if Student A is struggling with a subject and Student B has mastered it, why not let them connect? Our platform allows Student A to book a slot with Student B and get one-on-one help in understanding the concept.

Whether you're stuck on a tough assignment or preparing for an upcoming exam, StudyBuddy can help you find someone to guide you. It’s all about peer learning and mutual growth!

We believe every student has something to offer — so if you're good at a subject, you can also share your knowledge and help others while reinforcing your own skills.
